Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Shiji Daily is a daily historical wisdom app designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Shiji Daily does not collect any personal information. There are no analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party data sharing. Your viewing history and preferences are stored only on your device.

Network Access

Shiji Daily is an offline-first application. All historical content is bundled within the app. The only network access occurs during in-app purchases, which are processed entirely by Apple's App Store infrastructure.

In-App Purchases

Shiji Daily offers a one-time lifetime purchase processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple according to their privacy policy.

Data Storage

All app data (viewing history, preferences, trial status) is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms. No data is uploaded to any server.

Children's Privacy

Shiji Daily does not collect any data from any users, including children. The content is appropriate for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

隐私政策

最后更新：2026年4月

概述

史记日读是一款以隐私为核心原则设计的每日历史智慧应用。我们不收集、存储或传输任何个人数据。

数据收集

史记日读不收集任何个人信息。没有数据分析、没有用户追踪、没有广告 SDK、没有第三方数据共享。您的阅读历史和偏好设置仅存储在您的设备上。

网络访问

史记日读是一款离线优先的应用。所有历史内容都内置于应用中。唯一的网络访问发生在应用内购买时，该过程完全由苹果 App Store 基础设施处理。

应用内购买

史记日读提供一次性终身购买，通过苹果 App Store 处理。我们无法访问您的支付信息。所有交易均由苹果根据其隐私政策处理。

数据存储

所有应用数据（阅读历史、偏好设置、试用状态）均使用 iOS 标准存储机制存储在您的设备本地。没有任何数据上传到任何服务器。

儿童隐私

史记日读不收集任何用户的任何数据，包括儿童。内容适合所有年龄段。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更都将在应用内和本页面上以更新的修订日期反映。
